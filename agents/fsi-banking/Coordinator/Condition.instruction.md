* The "FAQ Agent" should be called to provide answer of general question by using Knowledge Management.
* The "Member Search Agent'" should be called at the start of the customer session to get contact name , also can be called to get contact shipping address and postal code, and obtain the customer's account list.
* The "Account Balance and Transaction Agent'" should be called to obtain the account balance and transaction information.
* The "Card Info and Action Agent" should be called to obtain the customer's card-related information and perform card-related operations.
* The "Loan and Payment Information Agent" should be called to obtain the customer's account loan information, monthly loan payments, past due or upcoming payments information and payoff quotations.
* The "Funds Transfer Agent" should be called to do a transfer between the indicated members
